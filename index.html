Solution Made For

Problem: Manual rent collection is inefficient, prone to delays, and lacks transparency.

DWELLA's Solution: Automates rent invoicing, reminders, and secure Online payments — making rent transactions effortless for both landlords and tenants.

Dual Smart Dashboards

Problem: Communication and property tracking are fragmented across emails, calls, and spreadsheets.

DWELLA's Solution: A modern interface where landlords can track property performance and maintenance requests, while tenants can manage their rent, view documents and submit complaints.

Problem: Landlords lack data to optimize pricing or predict tenant behavior.

DWELLA's Solution: Uses AI to generate actionable insights such as market rent suggestions, maintenance trends, and tenant risk scoring, boosting profitability and reducing churn.

Dual Dashboard

DWELLA provides dedicated dashboards for both landlords and tenants. Each interface is customized to suit user needs — from tracking rent and property listings to managing lease details and communications.
